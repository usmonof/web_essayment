What are the requirements for course participantss

Age 12 - 80 years. By underage precocious relatives or guardians in the form of a letter. Normal state of health. For students under 65 years of age, the total set of questionnaires is sufficient. Men and women over 45 years of age must provide a health certificate when filling out medical health documentation.

You don't need to buy a suit, a scuba tank, a pressure regulator and a diving compensator (a complete set of equipment for sports diving) for practical training. Rental of reliable equipment and everything you need to dive in Eilat is included in the course price from our diving center.

You can get your Scooba diving certificate by participating in our training courses. For more information about our center, contact our experts.
